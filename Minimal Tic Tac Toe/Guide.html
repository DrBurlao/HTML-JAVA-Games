Welcome to Three In A Row

Three In A Row is a simple tic-tac-toe game where you compete against the computer. Follow these steps to play Three In A Row:

How to Play:

  1. Make Your Move: Click on any empty cell on the game board to place your 'X'.
  2. Winning Condition: Try to align three of your symbols ('X') either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  3. Defeat the CPU: The computer ('O') will make its move after you. Defeat it by strategically placing your symbols.
  4. Restart the Game: If you want to start over, click the "Restart Game" button.
