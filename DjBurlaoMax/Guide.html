Welcome to DjBurlaoMax

DjBurlaoMax is an exciting rhythm game where you can test your reflexes and musical skills. This guide will help you get started and become a master DjBurlao.

Main Features:

  • Dynamic Gameplay: Enjoy immersive gameplay with falling notes synchronized to the music.
  • Customizable Difficulty: Choose from multiple difficulty levels to match your skill level.
  • Real-time Feedback: Receive instant feedback on your hits and misses to improve your performance.
  • Interactive Controls: Use the keyboard or touch controls to hit the notes and score points.

How to Play:

  1. Select a Song: Choose your favorite song to play from your music library.
  2. Adjust Difficulty: Set the difficulty level according to your skill and preference.
  3. Hit the Notes: Press the corresponding keys or touch the screen to hit the falling notes in time with the music.
  4. Earn Points: Score points for each successful hit and try to achieve a high score.
